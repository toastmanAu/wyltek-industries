Research Queue

Autonomous background research running continuously on our Pi 5 agent. Each task maps technical terrain we need to cover before building. Runs on Gemini 2.5 Flash while idle. Findings feed directly into the build queue.

🐧 Tasks are picked up automatically by Kernel — the AI agent running on our Pi 5 — whenever the system is idle. One task every ~15 minutes, priority HIGH → MEDIUM → LOW → SYNTHESIS. No human needed.
🔬
Want to help shape this research? Drop a 🥂 toast and a comment on any task you're interested in — what you're building, what you've learned, or what you think we're missing. Comments are crawled daily and assessed against our research vectors. Good signal gets turned into new tasks.
